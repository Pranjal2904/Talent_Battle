Bachelor of Computer Applications Honors

BCA (Hons) or Bachelor of Computer Applications Honors is an Under-Graduate Information Technology course. As far as computer application is concerned it is application software that is designed to help the user to perform singular or multiple related specific tasks. Examples include enterprise software, accounting software, office suites, graphics software, and media players. The Bachelor of Computer Applications Honours program is a three to four years duration course divided into many semesters.

The Bachelor of Computer Applications Honors degree program is an educational program that is also being conducted by many private institutes on both a regular and distance basis. There is a minor difference between the syllabus of BCA and BCA (Hons). So as far as an overall criterion regarding the course is concerned it totally depends on the thing that in which student is going to take admission.

BCA (Hons) Eligibility

Aspiring students should have completed their 10+2 examination from any relevant stream with English as a mandatory subject from a recognized Board. The candidates should have secured a minimum of 45% aggregate marks or equivalent at their Intermediate level.
