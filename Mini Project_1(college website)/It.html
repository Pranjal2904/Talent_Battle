B.tech in IT

B.Tech in Information Technology, or “B.Tech in IT” as it is widely known, is an undergraduate program in engineering with a specialization in the field of Information and Technology.

The duration of this degree course is generally 4 years, extendable up to 6 years in exceptional cases

The primary focus of study in this degree course is computer software development, though component-level knowledge of computer systems also comprises the curriculum

Candidates seeking admission in this course must have had Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics at 10+2 level from a recognized board. After passing the secondary education exam, one can appear for the entrance test conducted by certain universities or opt for a lateral entry admission to 2nd year of B.Tech in IT. Direct admissions are also offered by many private institutions.
