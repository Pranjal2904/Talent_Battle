ITM University, Gwalior Madhya Pradesh was established in the year 1997 as Private University Educational Institute to provide higher education to build a career in the field of Engineering, Management and Humanities. ITM University is approved by the reputed UGC, NCTE, COA, PCI, INC, BCI and ICAR, and is accredited by NAAC. Management of ITM University, Gwalior is working under Prof. S.S. Bhakar (Vice-Chancellor).

About ITM University Gwalior

ITM University came into existence back in 1997 with the support of Samata Lok Sansthan Trust (established in 1984) with the AICTE's clearance. It has been recognised by UGC, and approved by NAAC and ICAR. Architecture courses have their approval from CoA; nursing courses are approved by INC. Pharmacy courses has their approval from PCI, law courses from BCI, and Education courses are approved by NCTE.

Trust opened ITM (MCA) in 2000 whereas B.E. students were admitted to the Institute of Information Technology & Management (IITM) in 2002. The additional courses MBA & MCA were launched in 2006-2007 & 2007-2008, respectively. These organisations have received proper AICTE approval and are associated with RGPV, Bhopal. In the year 2000, the Institute of Allied Sciences & Computer Applications, and in 2002, the Nursing Science Studies were also introduced. ITM School of Law was founded in 2003, the Education college in 2002, and the Management School in 2008.These colleges have their affiliation with Jiwaji University in Gwalior.

ITM University is a multifunctional institution known for its research and instruction in all fields of study, including the sciences, management, fine arts, nursing, and other fields. The college has diploma courses in engineering, pharmacy, business, journalism, and Para medical sciences. It also offers UG and PG programs along with 5 years integrated programs like B.Tech-M.Tech, BA B.Ed, BA LLB/BBA LLB/ B.Com LLB (H). The college has Ph.D programs in computer applications, engineering subjects, management, agriculture, physical education, and pharmacy.

According to India Today's July 2019 ranking, it was ranked first in the best private universities in MP state and the only private university from MP to be placed in the Top 50 list in India. A variety of conferences, panels, forums, and workshops are held by ITM every year. These activities assist in having conversations between academics, business people, policymakers and the students.
